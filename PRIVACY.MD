Privacy Policy
Effective Date: May 5, 2026
1. Data Collection & Usage
"Custom 7TV Emote Menu by Int32" does not store or sell any personal data. To provide its core functionality, the extension automatically reads the Twitch channel name from the URL or page content when you visit twitch.tv.
This channel name is transmitted to the following third-party services:

* [DecAPI](https://decapi.me/): To resolve the Twitch username into a unique [User ID](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/User_identifier).
* [7TV](https://7tv.app/): To retrieve the [emote](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emote) set associated with that User ID.

This data is used strictly for the single purpose of fetching and displaying emotes and is not used for any other purpose.
2. Data Storage
No user data is saved on our servers or transmitted to any other third parties. All processing happens in real-time.
3. Third-Party Privacy
The data transmitted to DecAPI and 7TV is governed by their respective privacy policies. We do not control how these services handle the data they receive.
4. External Links
Our extension may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites.
