## Privacy Policy
Effective Date: May 8, 2026

## 1. Data Collection & Usage
"Custom 7TV Emote Menu by Int32" collects and processes specific data to provide its core functionality:

* Channel Context: The extension reads the Twitch channel name from the URL or page content to fetch the correct emote sets.
* Authentication Data (New): If you choose to log in, the extension requests a Twitch Access Token via OAuth. This token is used exclusively to verify your identity with the Twitch API to retrieve your personal emotes and subscription status.
* Third-Party Transmission:
* Twitch API: Your token is sent to Twitch to authorize data requests.
   * DecAPI: Used to resolve Twitch usernames into unique User IDs.
   * 7TV: Used to retrieve the emote sets associated with User IDs.

This data is used strictly for the single purpose of fetching and displaying emotes and is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing or tracking.

## 2. Data Storage
* Twitch Tokens: Your authentication token is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. It is never uploaded to our servers. You can clear this data at any time by logging out or uninstalling the extension.You have full control over this data and can manually clear it at any time by clicking the "Log Out" button within the extension.
* Real-time Processing: No other user data is saved on our servers. All other processing happens in real-time within your browser.

## 3. Third-Party Privacy
The data transmitted to Twitch, DecAPI, and 7TV is governed by their respective privacy policies. We do not control how these external services handle the data they receive.

## 4. Limited Use Disclosure
This extension complies with the Google Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not use your Twitch data for any purpose other than providing and improving the user-facing features of this extension.
